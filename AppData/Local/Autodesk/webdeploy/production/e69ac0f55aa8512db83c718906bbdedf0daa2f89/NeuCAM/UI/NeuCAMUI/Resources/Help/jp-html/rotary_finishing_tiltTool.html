Automatically tilts the tool around the rotary axis to avoid collisions between the shaft or holder and the part. If the collision cannot be avoided with 4-axis motion, the colliding part of the toolpath is trimmed away.

In the Tool tab, specify Shaft & Holder clearances so that collision avoidance can prevent the tool from getting too close to the part.

In the example below, the yellow arrow shows the direction of the passes, and the orange arrow shows the direction the tool tilts, which is around the rotary axis, to avoid the collision.
